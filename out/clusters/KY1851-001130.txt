KY1851-001130

102. In all cases of the return of service upon a summans by an officer, the
return must state the time of the service, and that a copy was delivered to or
offered and refused by the defendant. And if a return of service is defective
in these respects, the officer may be fined by the court. not exceeding ten
dollars, and shall be liable to the action of any person aggrieved by such
defect. But the court may permit an amendment, according to the truth of the
case. . ' ‘
