CA1851-001510

141. The Sheriff shall return the writ of attachment with the summons, if
issued at the same time; otherwise, within twenty days after its receipt, with
a certificate of his proceedings endorsed thereon, or attached thereto. The
provisions of this chapter shall not apply to any suits already commenced,
but so far as such suits may beconcerned, the Act entitled an .Act to regulate
proceedings against debtors by attachment, passed April 22d, 1850, shall be
deemed in full force and effect.
