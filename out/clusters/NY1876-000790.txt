NY1883-000780

74. Certain loans prohibited. [Amended by Ch. 542 op 1879.] An attorney or
counsellor shall not, by himself, or by or in the name of another person, either
before or after action brought, promise to give, or pro cure to be promised
or given, a valuable consideration to any person, as an inducement to placing,
or in consideration of having placed, in his hands, or in the hands of another
person, a demand of any kind, for the purpose of bringing an action thereon. But
this section does not apply to an agreement between attorneys and counselors, or

MT1895-001950

406. An attorney and counselor must not, by himself, or by or in the name of
another person, either before or after action brought, promise or give, or
procure to be promised or given, a valuable consideration to any person, as
an inducement to placing, or in consideration of having placed, in his hands,
or in the hands of another person, a demand of any kind, for the purpose of
bringing an action thereon. But this Section does not apply to an agreement
between attorneys and counselors, or either, to divide between themselves the
compensation to be received. An attorney and counselor who violates either of
the last two Sections, is guilty of a misdemeanor; and on conviction there­ of,
shall be punished accordingly, and must be removed from office by the Supreme
Court.
