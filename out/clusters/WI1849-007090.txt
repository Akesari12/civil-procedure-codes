WI1849-007090

In the cases in which, according to the provisions of this chapter, a defendant
at the commencement of a suit shall be entitled to require security for costs,
the attorney for the plaintifff shall be liable for such costs to an amount
not exceeding one hundred dollars, until security therefore be filed as herein
provided, whether such security shall have been required by the defendant or
not.
