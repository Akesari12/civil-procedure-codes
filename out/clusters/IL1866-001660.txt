IL1866-001660

Any executor, being a residuary legatee, may bring and maintain an action of
account against his coexecutor ; and any other residuary legatee shall have the
same remedy against executors and administrators. · Sze, 4. Actions of account
may be maintained by and against executors and administrators in all cases
in which the same might have been maintained by and against their testator or
intestate.
