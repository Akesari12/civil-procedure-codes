KY1851-005290

486. Where the judgment was rendered before the action stood for trial, the
suspension may be granted as provided in the last section, although no valid
defense to the action is shown; and the court shall make such orders concerning
the executions to be issued on the judgment, or on any replevy or forthcoming
bond taken under it, as shall give to the defendant the same rights of delay
that he would have had, if the judgment had been rendered at the proper time.
