KY1851-000660

58. In the case of an assignment of a thing in action, the action by the
assignee is without prejudice to any discount, set-off, or defense now allowed.
And where the assignment is not authorized by statute, the assignor must be
a party, as plaintifff or defendant. This section does not apply to bills of
exchange, nor to promissory notes placed upon the footing of bills of exchange,
nor to common orders or checks.
