OH1889-005120

5330. Bights of minors reserved. It shall not be necessary to reserve in a
judgment or order the right of a minor to show cause against it after attaining
the age of majority; but in any case in which, but for this sec tion, such
reservation would have been proper, the minor may, within one year after
arriving at the age of majority, show cause against such order or judgment. [83
v. 74 ; 51 v. 57, \ 386 ; S. & C. 1054.] See 45 O. S. 93 ; 17 O. S. 484.
