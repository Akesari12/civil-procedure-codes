CA1872-012230

1558. In case of a postponement, notice thereof must be given, by a public
declaration, at the time and place first appointed for the sale, and if the
postponement be for more than one day, further notice must be given, by posting
notices in three or more public places in the county where the land is situated,
or publishing the same, or both, as the time and circumstances will admit.
