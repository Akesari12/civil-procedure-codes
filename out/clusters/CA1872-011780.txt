CA1872-011780

1504. A judgment renderedagainst an executor or administrator, upon any claim
for money against the estate of his testator or intestate, only establishes
the claim in the same manner as if it had been allowed by the executor
or administrator and the executor or administrator pay in due course of
administration the amount ascertained to be due. A certified transcript of the
judgment must be filed in the Probate Court. No execution must issue upon such
judgment, nor shall it create any lien upon the property of the estate or give
to the judgment creditor any priority of payment.
