CA1850-002210

205. When the purchaser of any goods or chattels shall pay the purchase money,
the officer selling the same shall deliver him the property, and if desired,
shall execute an instrument of writing, drawn and delivered to him at the
expense of the purchaser, setting forth the sale and payment of the purchase
money, and conveying to the purchaser all the right, title, and interest which
the debtor had in and to the property sold on the day the execution was levied.
