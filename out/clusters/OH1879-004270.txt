OH1879-004270

6272. Any court of record of this state, or a judge thereof, may grant a
commission to take depositions within or without the state, which commission
must be issued by the clerk, and under the seal, of the court granting the
same; and the person or persons to whom it is issued must be named therein,
and depositions under it must be taken upon written interrogatories unless the
parties otherwise agree.
