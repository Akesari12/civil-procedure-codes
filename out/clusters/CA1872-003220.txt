NM1897-001060

105. When an action is against two or more defendants jointly or severally
liable on a contract, and the summons is served on one or more, but not all
of them, the plaintifff may proceed against the defendants served in the same
manner as if they were the only defendants, but the action shall not abate as to
other defendants, and shall proceed to final judgment against any or all of such
other defendants, and when necessary more than one judgment may be rendered in
the same action.
