NY1850-020330

1861. The person to whom a tender is made, must at the time specify any
objection he may have to the money, instrument or property, or he must be deemed
to have waived it, and if the objection be to the amount of money, the terms of
the instrument, or the amount or kind of property, he must specify the amount,
terms or kind which he requires, or be precluded from objecting afterwards.
