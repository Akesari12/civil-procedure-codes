CA1872-012530

1597. When a person who is bound by -contract in Writing to convey any real
estate, dies before making the conveyance, and in all cases where such decedent,
if living, might be compelled to make such conveyance, the Probate Court may
make a decree authorizing and directing his executor or administrator to convey
such real estate to the person entitled thereto.
