OH1853-001880

166. A surrender of the defendant to the sheriff of the county in which he was
arrested, with a delivery of a certified copy of the undertaking of the bail,
whether such surrender be made by the defendant himself or by his bail, shall

the court, an exoneration of the bail shall be entered 'on his undertaking.
