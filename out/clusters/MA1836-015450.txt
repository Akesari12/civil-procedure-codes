MA1836-015450

If, at the time when any cause of action, mentioned in this chapter, shall
accrue against any person, he shall be out of the state, the action may be
commenced, within the time herein limited therefore, after such person shall
come into the state ; and if after any cause of action shall have accrued,
the person against whom it has accrued shall be absent from and reside out of
the state, the time of his absence shall not be taken as any part of the time
limited for the commencement of the action.
