CA1872-012150

1550. No sale of real estate at private sale shall be confirmed bythe Court,
unless the sum offered is at least ninety per cent of the appraised value
thereof, nor unless such real estate has been appraised within one year of the
time of such sale. If it has not been so appraised, or if the Court is satisfied
that the appraisement is too high or too low, appraisers must be appointed,
and they must make an appraisement thereof in the same manner as in case of an
original appraisement of an estate. This may be done at any time before the sale
or the confirmation thereof.
