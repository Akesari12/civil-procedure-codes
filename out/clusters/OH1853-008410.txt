OH1853-008410

63. The trial may be adjourned upon the application of either party, without
the consent of the other, for a period not exceeding thirty days as follows: The
party asking the adjournment must, if required by his adversary, prove by his
own oath, or otherwise, that he cannot for want of material testimony which he
expects to procure, safely proceed to trial.
