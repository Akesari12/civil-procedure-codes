NY1829-030060

The next of kin, legatees, heirs and devisees, may show that there are debts
of a prior class unsatisfied, or that there are unpaid debts of the same class
with that on which the suit is brought ; and if it appear that the value of the
personal property delivered to them, or of the real estate descended or devised
to them, does not exceed the debts of a prior class, judgment shall be rendered
in their favor.
