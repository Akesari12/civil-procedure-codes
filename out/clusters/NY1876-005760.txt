NY1876-005760

534. Where a cause of action, defense, or counterclaim, is founded upon an
instrument for the payment of money only, the party may set forth a copy of the
instrument, and state that there is due to him thereon, from the adverse party,
a specified sum, which he claims. Such an allegation is equivalent to setting
forth the instrument, according to its legal effect.
