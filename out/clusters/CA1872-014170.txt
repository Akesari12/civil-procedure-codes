CA1872-014170

1805. No action can be maintained against the sureties on any bond given
by a guardian, unless it be commenced within three years from the discharge
or removal of the guardian; but if at the time of such discharge the person
entitled to bring such action is under any legal disability to sue, the action
may be commenced at any time within three years after such disability is
removed.
