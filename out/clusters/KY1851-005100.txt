KY1902-005350

506 [567]. Revivor of real actions against heirs or devisees. —Upon the death
of a defendant in an action for the recovery of real property only, or which
concerns only his rights or claims to such property, the action may be revived
against his real representatives or any of them, and an order therefore may be
forthwith made in the manner directed in the preceding sections of this title.
This section does not apply to an action to enforce a vendor's lien on land. 14
Bush, 683-84.
