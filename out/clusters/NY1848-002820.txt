civil calendar shall be disposed of in the fol lowing order, unless, for the

tried by the court; (3) Issues of law. The court should be formally adjourned.
—When the judge presiding leaves a court finally before the term has expired,
he should have it adjourned and not leave it open to take care of itself. Such
practice has no legal sanction, and it gives rise to misapprehension, confusion
and wrong. Foley v. Blank, 92-476.
