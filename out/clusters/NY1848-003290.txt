OH1889-006970

5482. Debtor may pay execution against creditor. After the issue of execution
against property, a person indebted to the judgment debtor may pay to the
sheriff the amount of his debt, or so much thereof as may be necessary to
satisfy the execution ; and the sheriff's re ceipt shall be a sufficient
discharge for the amount so paid, or directed to be credited by the judgment
creditor on the execution. [51 v. 57, I 463 ; S. & C. 1089.] And the same rule
applies to an execution held by a constable and issued by a justice of the
peace, 15 O. S. 176.
