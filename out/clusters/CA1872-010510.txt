CA1872-010510

1339. No will shall be proved as a lost or destroyed will, unless the same is
proved to have been in existence at the time of the death of the testator, or is
shown to have been fraudulently destroyed in the lifetime of the testator, nor
unless its provisions are clearly and distinctly proved by at least two credible
witnesses.
