IA1851-003890

1909. When there are no bidders, or when the amount offered is grossly
inadequate, or when for any cause the sale is prevented from taking place on
the day fixed, the sheriff may postpone the sale for not more than three days
without being required to give any farther notice thereof, but he shall not make
more than two such postponements.
