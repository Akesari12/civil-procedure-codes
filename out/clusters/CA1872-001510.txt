CA1872-001510

182. If an application for an order, made to a. Judge of a Court in Which the
action or proceeding is pending, is refused, in Whole or in part, or is granted
conditionally, no subsequent application for the same order can be made to any
Court Commissioner, or any other Judge, except of a higher Court; but nothing
in this section applies to motions refused for any informality in the papers or
proceedings necessary to obtain the order.
