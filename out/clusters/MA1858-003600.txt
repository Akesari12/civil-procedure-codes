MA1858-003600

A judge before whom proceedings are pending. in relation to the estate of a
debtor may, upon the complaint under oath of any person interested therein,
against any one suspected of having fraudulently received, concealed, embezzled,
or conveyed away, any money, goods, elfects, or other estate, of the debtor,
cite the suspected person to appear before him and be examined on oath upon
the matter of the complaint. If the person cited shall refuse to appear and
submit to such examination or to answer such interrogatories as shall lawfully
be propounded to him, the judge may commit him to the jail of the county, there
to remain in close custody until he submits to the order of the court. All such
interrogatories and answers shall be in writing, signed by the party examined,
and filed in court.
