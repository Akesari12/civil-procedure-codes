NY1850-003440

312. The officer must thereupon, at least three days before the court, summon
each of the persons named in the list, by giving him the notice mentioned in
the last section personally, or by leaving it at his place of residence with
some person of suitable age and discretion; and must return the list to the
court, at its opening on the day for which the jury was drawn, specifying the
persons summoned and the manner in which each was notified. If the officer be a
policeman, he is not entitled to any fee for summoning the jury.
