NY1850-005620

515. If it be alleged by a party for whom an attorney appears, that he does so
without authority, the court may, at any stage of the proceedings, relieve the
party for whom the attorney has assumed to appear, from the consequences of
his acts. It may also summarily, upon motion, compel the attorney to repair the
injury to either party, consequent upon his assumption of authority.
