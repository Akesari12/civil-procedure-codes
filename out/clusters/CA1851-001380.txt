CA1851-001380

128. Any person owing debts to the defendant, or having in his possession,
or under his control, any credits or other personal property belonging to the
defendant, may be required to attend before the Court or Judge, and be examined
on oath respecting the same. The defendant may also be required to attend for
the purpose of giving information respecting his property, and may be examined
on oath. The Court or Judge may, after such examination, order personal property
capable of manual delivery, to be delivered to the Sheriff on such terms as may
be just, having reference to any liens thereon, or claims against the same, and
a memorandum to be given of all other personal property, containing the amount
and description thereof.
