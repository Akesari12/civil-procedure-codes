KY1851-003710

341. In an action by a mortgagee for the foreclosure of his mortgage, and sale
of the mortgaged property, a receiver may, in like manner, be appointed, where
it appears that the mortgaged property is in danger of being lost, removed,
or materially injured, or that the condition of the mortgage has not been
performed, and that the property is probably insufficient to discharge the
mortgage debt.
