CA1872-011020

1402. When it comes to his knowledge that the bond of any executor or
administrator is, from any cause, insufficient, the Probate Judge, without any
application, must cause him to be cited to appear and show cause why he should
not give further security, and must proceed thereon as upon the application of
any person interested.
