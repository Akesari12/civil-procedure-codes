IA1851-004470

1965. The writ of certiorari may be granted whenever ‘specially authorized
by law and also in all cases where an inferior tribunal, board, or officer
exercising judicial functions, is alledged to have exceeded their proper
jurisdiction or is otherwise acting illegally, when in the judgment of the court
applied to for the writ there is no other plain, speedy, and adequate remedy.
