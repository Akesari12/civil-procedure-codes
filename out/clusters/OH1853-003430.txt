OH1853-003430

307. The trial of an issue of fact, and the assessment of damages in any case,
shall be in the order in which they are placed on the trial docket, unless by
consent of the parties, or the order of the court, they are continued or placed
at the heel of the docket. The time of hearing all other cases shall be in the
order in which they are placed on the docket, unless the court in its discretion
shall otherwise direct. The court may in its discretion hear at any time a
motion, and may by rule prescribe the time for hearing motions.
