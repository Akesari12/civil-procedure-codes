CA1872-012350

1570. At any sale, under order of the Probate Court, of lands upon which there
is a mortgage or lien, the holder thereof may become the purchaser, and his
receipt for the amount due him from the proceeds of the sale is a payment pro
tanto. If the amount for which he purchased the property is insumcient to defray
the expenses and discharge his mortgage or lien, he must pay to the Court or the
Clerk thereof an amount sufiicient to pay such expenses.
