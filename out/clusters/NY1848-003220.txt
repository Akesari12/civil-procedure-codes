causes of arrest required by section 179. If any defendant be in actual custody
under an order of arrest, and the plaintifff shall neglect to enter judgment in
the action within one month after it is in his power to do so, or shall neglect
to issue execution against the person of such defendant, within three months
after the entry of judg ment, such defendant may, on his motion, be discharged
from custody by the court in which such action shall have been commenced, unless
good cause to the contrary be shown ; and, after being so discharged, such de
fendant shall not be arrested upon any execution issued in such action.
