NY1829-022680

Nothing herein contained shall be construed to authorize the entry of a judgment
against any party who shall have died before a verdict actually rendered against
him, notwithstanding he may have died on the first or any other day of the term
or sitting of the court at which such verdict shall have been taken; but such
verdict shall be absolutely void.
