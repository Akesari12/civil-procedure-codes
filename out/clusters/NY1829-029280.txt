NY1829-029280

The time which shall have elapsed between the death of any person, and the
granting of letters testamentary or of administration, on his estate, not
exceeding six months, and the period of six months after the granting of such
letters, shall not be deemed any part of the time limited by any law for the
commencement of actions, by execu· tors or administrators.
