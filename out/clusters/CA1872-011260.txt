CA1872-011260

1437. When such suspension is made, notice thereof must be given to the executor
or administrator, and he must be cited to appear and show cause why his letters
should not be revoked. If he fail to appear in obedience to the citation, or, if
appearing, the Court is satisfied that there exists cause for his removal, his
letters must be revoked, and letters of administration granted anew, as the case
may require.
