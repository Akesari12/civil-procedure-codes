OK1893-003650

11. Actions shall -be triable at the first term of the court after the issues
therein by the times fixed for pleadings are, or shall have been madc up ten
days before the term. When issues of law are made up either before or during
a term of court, but after the period for preparing the trial docket of such
term, the clerk shall place such actions on the trial docket of that term; and
when any demurrer shall be adjudged to be frivolous, the cause shall stand for
hearing or trial in like manner as if an issue of fact had been joined in the
first instance; but the court may, in its discretion, fix specially the time
when such cause shall stand for trial.
