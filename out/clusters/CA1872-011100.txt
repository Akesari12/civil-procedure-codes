CA1872-011100

1414. Before any letters issue to any special administrator, he must give bond
in such sum as the Probate Judge may direct, with sureties to the satisfaction
of the Judge, conditioned for the faithful performance of his duties; and he
must take the usual oath and have the same indorsed on his letters.
