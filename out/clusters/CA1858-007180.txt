CA1858-007180

665. Whenever costs are awarded to a party by an appellate court, such party
may have an execution for the same on filing a remittitur with the clerk of
the court below, and it shall be the duty of such clerk whenever the remittitur
is filed, to issue the execution upon application therefore ; and whenever
costs are awarded to a party by an order of any court, such party may have an
execution therefore in like manner as upon a judgment.
