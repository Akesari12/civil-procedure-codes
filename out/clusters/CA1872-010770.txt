CA1872-010770

1374. Any person interested may contest the petition, by filing written
opposition thereto, on the ground of the incompetency of the applicant, or may
assert his own rights to the administration and pray that letters be issued
to himself. In the latter case the contestant must file a petition and give
the notice required for an original petition, and the Court must hear the two
petitions together.
