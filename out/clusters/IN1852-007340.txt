IN1852-007340

750. The information may be filed by the prosecuting attorney in the circuit
court of the proper county upon his own relation, whenever he shall deem it his
duty to do so, or shall be directed by the court or other competent authority;
or by any other person on his own relation, whenever he claims an interest in
the office, franchise or corporation, which is the subject of the information.
