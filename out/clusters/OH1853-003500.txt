OH1853-003500

312. Any party to a civil action or proceeding may compel any adverse party,
or person for whose benefit such action or proceeding is instituted, prosecuted
or defended, at the trial or by deposition, to testify as a Witness in the same
manner, and subject to the same rules as other witnesses.

CT1908-001740

Adverse parties may be compelled to testify. Any party to a civil action may
compel any adverse party, or any person for whose immediate and adverse benefit
such action or proceeding is in stituted, prosecuted, or defended, to testify
as a witness in his behalf, in the same manner and subject to the same rules as
other witnesses.
