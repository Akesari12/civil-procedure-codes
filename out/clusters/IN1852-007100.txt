IN1852-007100

726. No person shall be discharged from an order of commitment issued by any
judicial or peace officer for want of bail or in cases not bailable, on account
of any defect in the charge or process, or for alleged want of probable cause;
but in all such cases the court or judge shall summon the prosecuting witnesses,
investigate the criminal charge, and discharge, let to bail, or recommit the
prisoner, as may be just and legal, and recognize witnesses when proper.
