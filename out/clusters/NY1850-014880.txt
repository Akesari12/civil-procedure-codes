NY1850-014880

1366. The statement may be filed with a county clerk, or with the clerk of the
superior court of the city of New-York, or the clerk of the court of common
pleas of the city of New-York, who must endorse upon it, and enter in the
judgment book, a judgment of the supreme or superior court or court of common
pleas, for the amount confessed, with five dollars costs. The statement and
affidavit with the judgment endorsed, thereupon become the judgment roll.
