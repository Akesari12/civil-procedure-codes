NY1876-005380

497. Upon the decision of a demurrer, either at a general or special term, the
court may, in its discretion, allow ' the party in fault to plead anew or amend,
upon such terms as are just. If a demurrer to a complaint is allowed, because
two or more causes of action have been improperly united, the court may, in its
discretion, and upon such terms as are just, direct that the action be divided
into as many actions, as are necessary for the proper determination of the
causes of action therein stated.
