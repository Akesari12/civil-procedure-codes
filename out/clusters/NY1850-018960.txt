NY1850-018960

1736. The effect of a judicial record of a sister state, is the same in this
state as in the state where it was made, except that it can only be enforced
here by an action or special proceeding, and except also, that the authority
of a guardian or committee, or of an executor or administrator, does not extend
beyond the jurisdiction of the government, under which he was invested with his
authority.
