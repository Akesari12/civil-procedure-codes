is of the age of fourteen years, or upwards; or, if he is under that age, upon
the application of his general or testamentary guardian, if he has one, or of a

471. An infant defendant must also appear by guardian, who must be a. competent
and responsible person, appointed upon the application of the infant, if he is
of the age of fourteen years, or upwards, and applies within twenty days after
service of the summons; or, if he is under that age, or neglects so to apply,
upon the application of any other party to the action, or of a relative or
friend of the infant. Where the application is made by a person, other than the

under that age, and within the State, to the person with whom he resides.
