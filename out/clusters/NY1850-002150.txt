NY1850-002150

193. No court can be opened, nor can any judicial business be transacted, on
Sunday, on the first day of January, on the fourth day of July, on Christmas
day, on a day on which a general election is held, or on a day appointed by
the governor of this state as a day of fast or thanksgiving, except for the
following purposes: 1. To give, upon their request, instructions to a jury then
deliberating on their verdict: 2. To receive a verdict, or discharge a jury: 3.
For the exercise of the powers of a single magistrate, in a criminal action, or
in a proceeding of a criminal nature.
