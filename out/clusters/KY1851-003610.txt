KY1851-003610

332. A motion to dissolve or modify an injunction granted by a county judge,
may, at any time, upon reasonable notice to the plaintifff, be made before the
judge of the court or any circuit judge, upon the plaintifff ’s petition and
affidavits alone. The order of the judge overruling the same, or dissolving
or modifying the injunction, with the notice, shall be returned to the office
of the clerk of the court. They shall be noted upon the record and the order
obeyed, as if made by the court.
