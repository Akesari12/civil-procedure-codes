TX1855-003320

281. The Sheriff may deliver any property attached to the person in whose
possession it was found, upon the execution in his presence of a bond to the
plaintifff by such person, for a sum equal to double the value of such property,
with one or more sufficient sureties, to the effect that the defendant shall
perform the judgement of the Court in the suit, or that the property or its
value shall be forthcoming and subject to the order of the Court for the
satisfaction of said judgment. In any proceeding on said bond it shall not be
defense that the property was not subject to the attachment.
