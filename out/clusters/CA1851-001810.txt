CA1851-001810

165. In charging the Jury the Court shall state to them all matters of law which
it thinks necessary for their information in giving their verdict; and if it
state the testimony of the case, it shall also inform the Jury that they are
the exclusive judges of all questions of fact. The Court shall furnish to either
party, at the time, upon request, a statement in writing of the points of law
contained in the charge; or shall sign, at the time, a statement of such points
prepared and submitted by the Counsel of either party.
