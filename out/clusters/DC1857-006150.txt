DC1857-006150

3. .Any person or hie personal representatives may maintain an action against
the executor or administrator of any decedent for any debt or personal demand
which might have been maintained against such decedent, and against the executor
or administrator of any testator or intestate who in his lifetime shall have
wasted, destroyed, taken, or carried away, or converted to hie own use the goods
and chattels of any such person, or committed any trespass or waste upon the
real estate of such person.
