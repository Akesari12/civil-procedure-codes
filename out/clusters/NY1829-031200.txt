NY1829-031200

Whenever any incorporated company shall have remained insolvent for one whole
year ; or for one year shall have neglected or refused to pay and discharge
its notes or other evidences of debt; or for one year shall have suspended
the ordinary and lawful business of such corporation ; it shall be deemed to
have surrendered the rights, privileges and franchises granted by any act of
incorporation, or acquired under the laws of this state, and shall be adjudged
to be dissolved. 22 .
