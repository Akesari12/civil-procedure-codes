12. In rendering judgment of foreclosure, the court shall give personal judgment
against any party to the suit liable upon any agreement or agreements, for the
payment of any sum or sums -of money secured by the mortgage, and shall order
the mortgaged premises, or so much thereof as may be necessary to satisfy the
mortgage and judgment and costs of the action, to be first sold, before levy
of execution upon other property of the defendant. The payment of the mortgaged
debt, with interest and costs, at any time before sale, shall satisfy the
judgment.
