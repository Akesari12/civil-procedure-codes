NY1850-016840

1543. Persons, capable of contracting, may submit to arbitration any
controversy, which might be the subject of a civil action between them, except
a question of title to real property, in fee or for life, or for an existing
term of more than twelve years. This qualification does not include questions
relating merely to the partition, or boundaries of real property, or to the
admeasurement of dower.
