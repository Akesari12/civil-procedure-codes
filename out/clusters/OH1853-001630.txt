in the same court, the de fendant may, on motion, and notice to the adverse
party, require him to show cause why the same shall not be consolidated ; and
if it appear that, at the time the motion is made, the actions could have been
joined, and if the court, or a judge thereof, find that they ought to be joined,
the several actions shall be consolidated. [51 v. 57, \\ 143, 144; S. & C. 990,
991.] 44 O. S. 346 ; 15 Bull 386. As where two actions are brought, one upon a
