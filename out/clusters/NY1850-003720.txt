NY1850-003720

339. When the appointment of referees is made by the court or a judge, the
referee must be, 1. Qualified as a juror, as provided in section 251; 2.
Competent as a juror between the parties: and 3. Not related by consanguinity or
affinity within the third degrees, to a judge by whom the court is held, or the
order made, or to one who is interested or connected as a partner with a person
so related.
