When the defendant is a minor under the age of fourteen years, the service must be upon him and upon his guardian, or father; or, if neither of these can be found, then
upon his mother, or the person having the care or control of
the infant, or with whom he lives. If neither of these can
be found, or, if the minor be more than fourteen years of
age, service on him alone, shall be squicient. The manner
of service may be the same as in the case of adults. '
