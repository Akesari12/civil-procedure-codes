When real estate has been taken in execution, it shall be sold at the county seat of the
county in which it is situated, and at the Court-house door, or at the door of the house in which the
County Court is usually held.

Notice of such sale shall be given as follows :

lst. A written or printed notice thereof shall be posted up at least twenty days before the sale in
these public places in the county. The notice shall intelliginy refer to the execution, and shall state
the time and place of the sale, and describe the property to be sold with common certainty by metes
and bounds, or by some other appropriate description.

2d. A copy of such notice shall be published for two successive weeks in some newspaper published
in the county, if there begone. .
