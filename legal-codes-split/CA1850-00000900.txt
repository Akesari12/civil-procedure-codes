undertaking of the bail in the court in which the action is pending, with his return endorsed, and shall

plaintifff, within ten days thereafter, may serve upon the Sheriff a notice that he does not accept the bail,
or he shall be deemed to have accepted it, and the Sheriff shall be exonerated from liability.
