When an officer, executor or administrator, within
this State, by misconduct or neglect Of duty, forfeits his bond
or renders his sureties liable, any person injured thereby, or
who is, by law, entitled to the benefit Of the security, may
bring an action thereon, in his own name, against the Oilicer,
executor, or administrator, and his sureties, to recover the
amount, to which he may be entitled, by reason Of the delinquency. The action may be instituted and proceeded in on
a certified copyl of the bond, which copy shall be furnished
by the person olding the original thereof.
