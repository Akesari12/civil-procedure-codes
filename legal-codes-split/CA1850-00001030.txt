The recovery of a judgment in the action by the plaintifff against the defendant, shall not
protect the plaintifff or the parties to the undertaking given to procure the order of arrest against the
claim of the defendant for damages, for wrongfully obtaining such order. In any such action for
damages against him, the plaintifff shall be bound to show not only that he had a good cause of action
against the defendant, but also that the facts and circumstances of the case justified the issuance of the
order.
