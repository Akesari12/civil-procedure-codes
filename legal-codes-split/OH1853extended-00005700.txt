The proceedings to vacate or modify the judgment or
order on the grounds mentioned in subdivisions four, five, six,
seven, eight and nine of section live hundred and thirty-four,
shall be by petition verified by aliidavit, setting forth the
judgment or order, the grounds to vacate, or modify it,
and the defense to the action, if the party applying was
defendant. On such petition a summons shall issue and be
served as in the commencement of an action.
