An execution. against the person of the debtor,
except as prescribed 'in, section four hundred and eighty-one,
can be issued only when the same is allowed by the supreme
court, the district court, the court of common pleas, or probate court, or any judge of either, upon being satisfied by
the affidavit of the judgment creditor or his attorney, and
such other evidence as may be presented of the existence of
one or more of the particulars mentioned in section four hundred and seventy-eight.
