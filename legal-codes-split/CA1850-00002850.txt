79. The Supreme Court may reverse, afiirm, or modify any judgment, order, or determination,
appealed from in whole or in part, and as to any or all of the parties, may grant new trials, and render
such judgment as substantial justice shall require, without regard to formal or technical defects, errors,
or imperfections, not affecting the very right and justice of the case. The Court shall review any
intermediate order involving the merits and necessarily affecting the judgment. When from the character
of the record no tangible point is presented for determination, the Court may remand the cause for a
new trial in the Court below. '
