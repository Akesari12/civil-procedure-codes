Whenever it shall appear to the satisfaction of a
justice, by proof made before him, that any 'person has been
duly served with a subpoena to appear and give testimony
before him in any matter in which he has authority to require
such witness to appear and testify, that his testimony is
material and that he refuses or neglects to attend as such
witness in conformity with such subpoena, the justice shall
issue a warrant to arrest the delinquent for the purpose of
compelling his attendance and punishing his disobedience.
