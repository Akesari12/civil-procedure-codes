It shall be the duty of every constable, on the receipt of any writ or_ other process, (subpoenas excepted,) to
note thereon the time of receiving the same ; he shall also
state in hi return on the same, the time and manner of executing it.
_ § 196. No constable shall make a return on any process

of “ Not found,” as to any defendant, unless he shall have
been once at least to the usual place of residence of the defendant, if such defendant have any in the county.
