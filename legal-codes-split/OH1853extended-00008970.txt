A constable is liable to the party in whose favor
an execution issued to him for the amount thereof, in the following cases :

1. Where he suffers thirty days to elapse without making
a true return thereof to the justice, and paying to him, or to
the party entitled, the money collected thereon by him.

2. Where he willfully and carelessly omits to levy on property within thirty days, or if the defendant be liable to be
imprisoned, then to arrest and commit him to the jail of the.
county within thirty days.
