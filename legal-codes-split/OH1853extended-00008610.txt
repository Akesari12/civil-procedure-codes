'If the suit be not continued, place of trial changed,
or neither party demand a jury upon the return day of the
summons, the justice shall try the cause; and if, after hearing
the evidence, he shall conclude that the complaint is not
true, he shall enter judgment against the plaintifff for costs ;
if he find the complaint true, he shall render a general judgment against the defendant and in favor of the plaintifff for
restitution of the premises and costs of suit; if he find the
complaint true in part, he shall render a judgment for the
restitution of such part only, and the costs shall be tax ed as
the justice shall deem just and equitable.
