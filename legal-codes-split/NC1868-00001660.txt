—Clerk to enter judgments on judgment book, also judgments rendered
In other courts, and index them,

'The Clerk of the Superior Court shall enter every judgment of the court, on his judgment book; it shall specify
clearly the relief granted, or other determination of the
action; he shall also enter on said book all judgments rendered in any other court, and authorized by law to be so
entered, and shall keep an alphabetical index of the whole,
with ‘thedates and numbers thereof.
