XII. MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS RESPECTING SHERIFFS AND THEIR OFFICERS.

Directions of party or attorney, not to excuse sheriff, unless in writing.
When sheriff committed for not paying over money, the fact to be reported to the governor.
Compensation to sheriff for services to the state.
Sheriff, how to common jury of inquest.
Sheriff, justified by process regular on its face.
Officer executing warranting, &c., bound to show it.
Disabilities of sheriff and his officers.
