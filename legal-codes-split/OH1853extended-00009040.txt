§.180. In all cases where any lands may [have] been let,
reserving rent in kind, and when the crops or emblements
growing or grown thereon, shall be levied on or attached, by
virtue of any execution, attachment or other process, against
the landlord or tenant, the interest of such landlord or tenant
against whom such process was not issued, shall not be affected thereby; but the same may be sold, subject to the
claim or interest of the landlord or tenant against whom such
process did not issue.
