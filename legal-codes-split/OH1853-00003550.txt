Any court of record of this State, or any judge
thereof, is authorized to grant a commission to take depositions within or without the State. The commission must be
issued to a person or persons therein named, by the clerk,
under the seal of the court granting the same, and depositions under it, must be taken upon written interrogatories,
unless the parties otherwise agree.
