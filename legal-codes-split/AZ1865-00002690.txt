After completing a sale of the property, or any part thereof
ordered to be sold, the referees shall report the same to the court, with a
description of the different parcels of land sold to each purchaser; the name
of the purchaser; the price paid or secured; the terms and conditions of
the sale; and the securities, if any, taken. The report shall be filed in the
office of the clerk of the county Where the property is situated.
