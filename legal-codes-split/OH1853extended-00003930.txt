If the taking of an account, or the proof of afact,
or the assessment of damages be necessary to enable the
court to pronounce judgment upon a failure to answer, or
after adecision of an issue of law, the court may with the assent of the party not in default, take the account, hear the
proof, or assess the damages; or may, with the like assent,
refer the same to a referee or master commissioner, or may
direct the same to be ascertained or assessed by a jury.
If a jury be ordered, it shall be on or after the day on which
the action is set for trial.
