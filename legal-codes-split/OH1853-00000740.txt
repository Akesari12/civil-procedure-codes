A summons against a corporation, may be served
upon the president, mayor, chairman of the board of directors or trustees, or other chief officer; or,.if its chief officer
is not found in the county, upon its cashier, treasurer,
secretary, clerk, or managing agent; or, if none of the
aforesaid officers can be found, by a copy left at the office,
or usual place of business of such corporation, with the
person having charge thereof.
