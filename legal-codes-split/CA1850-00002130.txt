When any right or shares of stock in any company 'or corporation shall be sold, the officer
making the sale shall execute an instrument in writing, to be drawn at the expense of the purchaser,
reciting the sale and payment of the consideration, and conveying to the purchaser such rights and
shares, and shall also leave with the cashier, secretary, or chief clerk or other agent of such corporation
or company, a copy of the execution and his return thereon, .and the purchaser shall thereupon be
entitled to all dixidends and stocks," and the same privileges as a member of such company or
corporation as such debtor was entitled to.
