After the issuing of an execution against property,
and upon proof by the affidavit of the judgment creditor or
otherwise, to the satisfaction of the court of common pleas,
or a judge thereof, or aprobate judge of the county in which
the order may be served, that the judgment debtor has property which he unjustly refuses to apply towards the satisfaction of the judgment, such court or judge may, by order, require the judgment debtor to appear at a time and place in
said county to answer concerning the same. And such proceedings may thereupon be had for the a plication of the
property of the judgment debtor towards e satisfaction of
the judgment as are prescribed in this chapter.
