The examination shall be byquestion and answer, unless the
parties otherwise agree. The deposition, when completed, shall be carefully read to and subscribed by the witness, then certified by the judge,
and immediately thereafter filed and recorded in the office of the clerk of
the county where it was taken ; together with the order for the examination of the witness, the affidavit on which the same was granted, and the
affidavit of service of the affidavit, order, or notice.
