Upon an affidavit that any person, or corporation, or company, has property of any judgment
debtor, or is indebted to him, the judge may by an order require such person, or corporation, or
company, or any olIicer or member thereof, to appear at a specified time and place, and answer
concerning the same. The judge may also in his discretion require notice of such proceeding to be
given to any party to the action, in such manner as may seem to him proper.
