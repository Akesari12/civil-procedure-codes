Before entering upon the duties of his office, a
master commissioner must be sworn to perform them faith—
fully, and with one or more sureties approved by the court,
execute an undertaking to the State of Ohio, in such sum as
the court shall direct, to the effect that he will pay over all
monies and faithfully discharge the duties of his office. The
undertaking shall be filed in the office of the county auditor,
and may be sued upon as provided in section five hundred
and sixty—six.
