Mun—Arrest, in what cases.

The defendant may be arrested in the following cases:

1. When the defendant has been guilty of a fraud in contracting the debt or obligation for which the action is
brought. ~

2. .When the defendant is not a resident of the State, or ~
is about to remove therefrom. _

3. When the defendant has removed or disposed of his

property, or is about to do so, with intent to defraud his
creditors.
