Any person against whom judgment may be rendered under the provisions of this act, except as hereinafter
excepted, may have stay of execution for the several periods
hereinafter mentioned, by entering into an undertakingto

the adverse party, within ten days after the rendition of such
judgment, with good and sufficient surety, resident of the
county, as the justice shall approve, conditioned for the payment of the amount of such judgment, interest and costs,
and costs that may accrue ; which undertaking shall be entered on the docket of the justice, and be signed by the surety.
