Upon the trial of any cause for the recovery of
property in specie, the jury must, if they find for the plaintifff, if practicable, assess the value of each article of the propertyseparately, and also assess damages for its detention; if
ey find for the defendant, they must in like manner assess
its value; and if in the possession of the plaintifff, assess the
damages for its detention. Judgment against either party
must be for the property sued for, or its alternate value,
with damaoes for the detention to the time of trial.
