The judges of the supreme court shall, during the
month of the first January after this code shall take effect, and
every two years thereafter, meet at the capitol of the State
and revise their general rules, and make such amendments
thereto as may be necessary to carry into effect the provisions of' this code: and they shall make such further rules
consistent therewith, as they may deem proper. The rules
so made shall apply to the supreme court, the district courts,
and the courts of common pleas.
