The sureties must be residents of the county in which the judgment is rendered, and each
must be worth of property within the county the amount specified in the undertaking, or the whole
number of sureties, if more than two, must be worth of such property in the aggregate double such
amount. An undertaking upon an appeal shall be of no effect unless it be accompanied by the affidavit
of the sureties that they have the qualifications required by this section. '
