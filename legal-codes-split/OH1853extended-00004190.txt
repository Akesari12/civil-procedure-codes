No action pending in any court, shall abate by the
death of either or both the parties thereto,except an action
for libel, slander, malicious prosecution, assault, or assault
and battery, for a nuisance, or against a justice of the peace
for misconduct in office, which shall abate by the death of the
defendant. . TITLE XIII.
