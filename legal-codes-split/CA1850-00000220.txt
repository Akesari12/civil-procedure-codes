Actions for the following causes must be tried in the county in which the subject of the action
or some part thereof is situated, subject to the power of the Court to change the place of trial in the
cases provided by statute.

lst. For the recovery of real property, or of an estate or interest therein, or for the determination in
any form of such right or interest, and for injuries to real property.

2d. For the partition of real property.

3d. For the enforcement of a mortgage of real property.
