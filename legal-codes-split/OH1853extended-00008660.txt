The officer shall, within ten days after receiving
the writ, execute the same by restoring the plaintifff to the
possession of the premises, and shall levy and collect the
costs and make return, as upon other executions. If the
officer shall receive a notice from the justice that the proceedings have been stayed, by an allowance of a writ of
error, he shall immediately delay all further proceedings
uponthe execution ; and if the premises have been restored
to the plaintifff, he shall immediately place the defendant
in the possession thereof, and return the writ with his pro.
ceedings and costs taxed thereon.
