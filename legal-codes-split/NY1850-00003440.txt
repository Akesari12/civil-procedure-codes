If the person so served, fail to appear or to show by proof, as prescribed in section 291, that he is exempt, his name must be immediately returned to the county clerk, and to the clerk of the justice’s court, in the district in which he resides, in the manner provided in sections 293 and 294, in respect to the original trial or justice’s jury list; and a ballot containing the name must be prepared and deposited in the undrawn jury box, in the manner provided in section 296.
