Where notice of a motion is required it must be
in writing, and shall state the names of the parties to the action or proceeding in which it is made, the name of the court
or judge before whom it is to be made, the place where and
the day on which it will be heard, the nature and terms of
the order or orders to be applied for, and if affidavits are to
be used on the hearing, the notice shall state that fact. It
shall be served a reasonable time before the hearing.
