The plaintifff, or his attorney, may object to the
bail for insufficiency, at any time within ten days after
the undertaking of the bail has been given; within such
time he shall serve upon the sheriff a written notice that
he does not accept the bail, or he shall be deemed to have
accepted it, and the sheriff shall be exonerated from
liability. When the undertaking is given alter the return
of the order of arrest, the plaintifff shall have notice
thereof.
