A_ husband shall not be a witness for or against his wife, nor a
wife a witness for or against her husband; nor can either, during the
marriage, or afterwards, be, without the consent of the other, examined as to
any communication made by one to the other during the marriage. But this
exception shall not apply to an action or proceeding by one against the
other.

SEC._398. An attorney or counsellor shall not, without the consent of
 client, be examined as, a witness as to any communication made by- the

 

client to him, or his advice given thereon, in the course of professional
employment.
