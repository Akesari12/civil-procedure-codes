The court or any judge thereof during vacation,
may, on the application of the plaintifff, and on good cause
shown, appoint a receiver, who shall take an oath faithfully
to discharge his duty, and shall give an undertaking to the
State of Ohio, in such sum as the court or judge may direct,
and with such security as shall be approved by the clerk of
such court, for the faithful performance of his duty, as such
receiver, and to pay over all money and account for all
property, which may come into his hands by virtue of his
appointment, at such times and in such manner as the court
may direct.
