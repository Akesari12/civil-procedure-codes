If the injunction be granted by a County Judge without notice, the defendant, at any time
before the trial, may apply, upon notice to a Judge of the Court of the county in which the action is
brought, to vacate or modify the same. The application may be made upon the complaint and the

affidavit upon which the injunction was granted, or upon affidavits on the part of the defendant, with or

' without the answer. .
