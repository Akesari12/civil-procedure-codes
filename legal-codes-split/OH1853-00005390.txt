Notices of motions mentioned in this chapter may
be served by a sheriff, coroner or constable, or by any disinterested person, and the return of any such officer, or affidavit of any such person, shall be proof of service. The service shall be on the party or his attorney of record, if the
said arty or his attorney be resident within the county in
whic the motion is made; and in case there is more than
one party adverse to such motion, service shall be made upon
each party or his attorney.
