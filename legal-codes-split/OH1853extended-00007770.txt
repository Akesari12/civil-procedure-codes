An order of attachment binds the property attached
from the time of service, and the garnishee hall stand liable
to the plaintifff in attachment for all property, monies and
credits in his hands, or due from him to the defendant from
the time he is served with the written notice mentioned in
section thirty-seven, but when property is attached in the
hands of a consignee or other person having a prior lien, his
liens thereon shall not be affected by the attachment.
