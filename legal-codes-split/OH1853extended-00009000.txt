All property taken in execution under the provisions of this act, shall be advertised for sale, at four of the most
public places within the township where such property was
seized, at least ten days previous to the time appointed for
such sale, which sale shall be held between the hours of ten
o’clock, A. M., and four o’clock, P. M., at the house, or on
the premises, where such property was taken, or at one of x
the most public places within the township.
