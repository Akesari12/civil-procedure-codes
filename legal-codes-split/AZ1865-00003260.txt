An undertaking upon an appeal shall be of no effect imless it
be accompanied by the affidavit of the sureties that they are each worth
double the amount specified therein, over and above all their just debts and
liabilities, exclusive of property exempt from execution. The adverse party
may, however, except to the sufficiency of the sureties within five days
after the filing of the undertaking; and unless they or other sureties justify
before a judge of the court below,or the district clerk, within five days
thereafter (upon notice to the adverse party), the appeal shall be regarded

as if no such undertaking had been given.
