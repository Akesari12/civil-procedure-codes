The provisions of this title subsequent to section
533, shall apply to the supreme court, and probate court, so
far as the same may be applicable to the judgments, or final
orders of such courts. The parties shall be limited to the
same time in which to commence proceedings, and in estimating time, the Probate Court shall, for this purpose, be considered as holding, in each year, three terms of four months
each, the first commencing on the first of January of each

year.
