Actions shall be triable at the first term of the

court, after the issues therein, by the times fixed for pleading, are or should have been made up; and when, by the times
fixed for pleading, the issues are, or should have been made
up during a term, such actions shall be triable at that term.
When the issues are, or should have been made up, either
before, or during a term of court, but after the period for
preparing the trial docket of such term, the clerk, if required
by the court, shall place such actions on the trial docket of.
that term. 5'
