An action other than one of those mentioned in the
first three sections of this chapter, against a non-resident of
this State or a foreign corporation, may be brought in any
county in which there may be prOperty of, or debts owing to,
said defendant, or where said defendant may be found; but
if said defendant be a foreign insurance company, the action
may be brought in any county, where the cause or some part
thereof, arose.
