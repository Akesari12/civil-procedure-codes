Either party or his attorney, if required, shall deliver to the other party or his attorney a copy of any deed,
instrument or other writing, whereon the action or defense is .
founded, or which he intends to offer in evidence at the trial.
If the plaintifff or defendant shall refuse to furnish the copy
or copies required, the party so refusing shall not be permitrted to give in evidence at the trial the original, of which a
copy has been refused. This section shall not apply to any
paper, a copy of which is filed with a pleading as provided
in section one hundred and seventeen.
