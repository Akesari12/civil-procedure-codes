The proceeds of the sale of the incumbered property shall be
applied under the direction of the court as follows:

1st. To pay its just proportion of the general costs of the action;
2d. To pay the costs of the reference;

3d. To satisfy and cancel of record the several liens in their order of
priority, by payment of the sums due and to become due; the amount due
to be verified by affidavit at the time of payment.

4th. The residue among the owners of the property sold, according to
their respective shares therein.
