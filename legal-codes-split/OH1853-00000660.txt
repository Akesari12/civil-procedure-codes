Where the time for bringing parties into court is
not fixed by statute, the summons shall be returnable on
the second Monday after the day of its date; but where
it is issued to any other than the county, in which the
action is brought, it may be made returnable, at the 0 tion
of the party having it issued, on the third or fourth onday after its date. It shall state' the day of the month on
which it is returnable.
