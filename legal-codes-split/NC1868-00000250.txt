In every action for the recovery of real preperty, or the
possession thereof, or damages for a trespass on such possession, the person establishing a legal title to the premises, shall
be presumed to have been possessed thereof within the time
required by law; and the occupation of such premises by any
other person shall be deemed to have been under and in subordination. to the legal title, unless it appears that such premises have been held and possessed adversely to such legal title,‘
for the time prescribed by law before the commencement of

such action.
