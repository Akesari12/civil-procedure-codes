The final orders, or decrees of courts of chancery
heretofore rendered, or which may hereafter be rendered in
any chancery proceeding pending at the time this code takes
effect, may be reviewed in the same manner, and within the
same time, as if this code had not taken effect; and all suits
in chancery, pending at that time, maybe prosecuted to final
decree in like manner.
