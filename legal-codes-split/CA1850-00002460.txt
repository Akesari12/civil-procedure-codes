When any party to an action desires to take the testimony of a witness residing out of the
county in which the action is pending, he may file his interrogatories in writing propounded to the
witness, and Serve upon the opposite party or his attorney a notice that such interrogatories have been
filed. The notice shall state the name and residence of the witness, and the title of the action in which
the answers will be used. A copy thereof shall be served by the Sheriff, who shall return and file in
court the original notice with his Certificate endorsed thereon, showing the time and manner of service.
