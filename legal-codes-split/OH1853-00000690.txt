The summons shall be served by the officer to
whom it is directed, who shall endorse on the original
writ, the time and manner of service. It may, also, be
served by any person not a party to the action, appointed
by the officer to whom it is directed. The authority of
such person shall be endorsed on the writ. When the
writ is served by a person appointed by the officer to
whom it is directed, or when the service is made out of
this State, the return shall be verified by oath or affirmation. '
