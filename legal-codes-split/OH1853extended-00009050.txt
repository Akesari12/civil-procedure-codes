In cases where the constable shall make it appear
to the satisfaction of the justice, that he has been deprived of
an opportunity of levying an execution within the time prescribed by this act, or otherwise prevented from making the
whole of the money therein required to be made, and shallmake return to the justice who issued the same to that effect,
such justice is hereby authorized and required to issue further process of execution, for the amount or balance remaining unsatisfied; which shall be served and returned, in .all
respects, as other executions are under this act.
