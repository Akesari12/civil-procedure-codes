The writ of review shall command the party to whom it is
directed to certify fully to the court issuing the writ, at a Specified time and

lace, and annex to the writ a transcript of the record and proceedings
{describing or. referring to them, with convenient certainty), that the same
may be reviewed by the court; and requiring the party in the meantime
to desist from further proceedings in the matter to be reviewed.
