Where several actions are brought on one bill of
exchange, promissory note, or other obligation or instrument
in writing, against several parties, who might have been
joined as defendants in the same action, as allowed by section thirty-eight, no costs shall be recovered by the plaintifff
in more than one of such actions, if the parties proceeded
against in the other actions, were at the commencement of
the previous action, openly within the State.
