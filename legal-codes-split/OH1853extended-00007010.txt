Every plea shall be oral, and shall be entered
on the minutes of the court in substantially the following
form:

1. If the defendant plead guilty-“ the defendant pleads
that he is guilty of the offense charged against him :”

2. If he plead not guilty—“ the defendant pleads that he
is not guilty of the offense charged against him :”

3. If he plead a former conviction or acquittal—“ the defendant pleads that he has already been convicted [or acquitted, as the case may be] of the offense charged against
